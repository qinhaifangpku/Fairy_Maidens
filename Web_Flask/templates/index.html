  • The charm of the data on Human

    --Analysis of physical and psychological health of Internet workers

  • BACKGROUND

    This project is based on data from hacker news. As we know, the main topic of hacking news is around the Internet industry, thus we found some interesting things about the physical and mental health of Internet workers from this datasets. As a programmer, because of the busy work, we sometimes forget to pay attention to our physical and psychological health development. Behavioral psychology studies have shown that a person's physical and mental health can be reflected from the daily behavior and comments of some of the emotions. The data set from the hacker news contains a large number of Internet workers’ comments and their schedule. We have excavated some interesting relationships between the development of physical and psychological health.

    STATISTICS

    We anayzed and filtered about 3.32 million comments and got the 78676 users who was actived in the latest year. We completed some experiments with the filtered data and got the personalized analysis of them.
